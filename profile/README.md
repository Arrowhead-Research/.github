# Arrowhead Research

**From Field to Function**

Arrowhead Research empowers military service-members to develop technical skills through hands-on engineering. We bridge tactical expertise with innovation by providing engineering experience, direct mentorship, and access to high-stakes technical challenges.

## Our Mission

Transform passion for technology into real-world impact by enabling military personnel to take mission-driven problems from concept through solution.

## What We Do

### Engineering Sprints

Build functional prototypes on defined timelines addressing mission-focused problems. Our repositories showcase projects developed by service-members tackling real operational challenges.

### Academia & Industry Partnerships

Engage with universities, research institutions, and industry partners to facilitate ongoing learning and knowledge exchange.

### Technical Communication

Develop and deliver polished technical work demonstrating disciplined engineering practices. Our open-source projects reflect our commitment to quality and collaboration.

### Fielding & Competition

Validate systems through events including Black Relay, Moonshot Rodeo, and national engineering challenges that test performance under real-world constraints.

## Get Involved

We welcome collaboration from:

- Military service-members interested in developing technical skills
- Industry partners looking to mentor and support mission-driven innovation
- Academic institutions engaged in defense-related research
- Contributors passionate about solving real-world operational challenges
